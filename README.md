# Electronics Diagnostics and Laboratory Testing

This repository documents practical procedures and techniques used in electronic product diagnostics and laboratory testing.

## Technical Activities

- Functional testing of electronic equipment
- Electrical measurements using laboratory instruments
- Failure diagnosis in electronic products
- Technical analysis of electronic circuits
- Support for product evaluation and validation

## Instruments Used

- Digital Multimeter
- Oscilloscope
- Power Meter
- Variac (AC voltage control)
- Soldering and Rework Station

## Typical Test Procedures

### Voltage Measurement
Measurement of input and internal voltages using a digital multimeter to verify circuit operation.

### Signal Analysis
Use of oscilloscope to analyze waveform behavior in electronic circuits.

### Power Consumption Test
Measurement of electrical consumption using power meter.

### Functional Test
Verification of product operation under normal conditions.

- Electronic troubleshooting
- Measurement and instrumentation
