# Electronic Measurement Techniques

## Objective
Document measurement techniques used in electronic equipment testing.

## Instruments

- Multimeter
- Oscilloscope
- Power meter
- Variac

## Measurement Types

### Voltage Measurement
Verification of DC and AC voltages in power circuits.

### Current Measurement
Verification of current consumption in electronic equipment.

### Signal Analysis
Observation of waveform characteristics using oscilloscope.

### Power Consumption
Measurement of energy consumption using power meter.

## Applications

- Functional verification
- Troubleshooting
- Performance analysis
