# Electronic Product Testing

## Objective

Perform functional and electrical tests on electronic products to verify performance and compliance.

## Test Types

### Functional Test
Verification of correct product operation.

### Electrical Safety Test
Verification of electrical parameters and voltage stability.

### Performance Test
Evaluation of product performance under operating conditions.

### Thermal Behavior
Monitoring temperature behavior during operation.

## Instruments Used

- Multimeter
- Oscilloscope
- Power meter
- Temperature measurement devices

## Skills Demonstrated

- Product evaluation
- Test procedures
- Data analysis
- Technical documentation
