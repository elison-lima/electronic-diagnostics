# Electronic Equipment Test Procedure

## Objective
Verify the electrical and functional operation of electronic equipment using laboratory instruments.

## Equipment Required

- Digital multimeter
- Oscilloscope
- Power meter
- Variac
- Rework station (if repair is required)

## Test Steps

1. Visual inspection of PCB and components.
2. Verification of input voltage.
3. Measurement of internal voltages.
4. Functional test of the equipment.
5. Signal analysis using oscilloscope when necessary.
6. Measurement of electrical consumption.

## Expected Result

The equipment must operate within normal electrical parameters and show stable signals and voltage levels.
