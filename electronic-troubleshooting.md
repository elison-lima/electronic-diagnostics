# Electronic Circuit Troubleshooting

## Objective

Identify faults in electronic equipment through systematic diagnostic procedures and measurement techniques.

## Troubleshooting Process

### 1. Visual Inspection

Initial inspection of the PCB to identify:

- Burnt components
- Broken solder joints
- Oxidation
- Damaged connectors

### 2. Power Verification

Confirm the correct supply voltage is reaching the circuit.

### 3. Signal Verification

Use oscilloscope to analyze signals in key points of the circuit.

### 4. Component Testing

Check critical components such as:

- Diodes
- Transistors
- Capacitors
- Resistors

### 5. Functional Testing

Operate the equipment to verify correct behavior.

## Tools Used

- Digital multimeter
- Oscilloscope
- Power meter
- Variac
- Soldering station
- Rework station

## Skills Demonstrated

- Electronic diagnostics
- Measurement instrumentation
- Circuit analysis
