# Multimeter Diagnostic Techniques

## Objective

Use a digital multimeter to verify electrical parameters and assist in troubleshooting electronic equipment.

## Equipment

- Digital multimeter
- Test probes
- Device under test (DUT)

## Measurements Performed

### Voltage Measurement

Verification of DC or AC voltages in power supply circuits and internal circuit nodes.

### Resistance Measurement

Measurement of component resistance to identify open circuits or damaged components.

### Continuity Test

Verification of electrical continuity in PCB tracks and connections.

### Diode Test

Checking semiconductor components such as diodes and rectifier bridges.

## Diagnostic Applications

Multimeter measurements are commonly used to detect:

- Open circuits
- Short circuits
- Power supply failures
- Incorrect voltage levels
- Damaged electronic components

## Typical Troubleshooting Steps

1. Perform visual inspection.
2. Measure input voltage.
3. Verify internal power rails.
4. Check continuity of PCB tracks.
5. Test critical components.
